Mix Affinity

Upload audio files and start analyzing

Mix Affinity is an audio analysis platform that lets producers compare their track against a professional reference in real time. It extracts mix-level features like loudness, low-end energy, stereo width, transients, and spatial balance, then visualizes the statistical differences clearly so you can see exactly where your mix diverges. Instead of guessing with your ears alone, collect objective insights into what makes a track feel polished versus amateur.

Features

Spatial Fingerprint

Visualize stereo field positioning, presence, and spatial characteristics of your audio tracks with interactive scatter plots.

Loudness Analysis

Measure and compare LUFS levels to ensure your tracks meet industry standards and maintain consistent loudness across your mix.

Crest Factor

Analyze dynamic range and peak-to-average ratios for optimal mastering decisions and better understanding of your track's dynamics.

Low-End Analysis

Examine sub-bass frequencies and low-end energy distribution across your tracks to ensure proper balance and clarity.

Turning the Abstract Into Something Measurable

MixAffinity doesn't tell you what's right or wrong.

It shows you how your mix behaves — compared to the reference you chose.

Where loudness shifts.
Where density changes.
Where low end is stable — or drifting.
Where width expands — or collapses.

The goal isn't to replace judgment.
It's to remove guesswork.
